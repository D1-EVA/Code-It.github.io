214. Shortest PalindromeHard2357189Add to ListShareYou are given a string s. You can convert s to a palindrome by adding characters in front of it.

Example 1:
Input: s = "aacecaaa"
Output: "aaacecaaa"
Example 2:
Input: s = "abcd"
Output: "dcbabcd"


Constraints:

0 <= s.length <= 5 * 104
s consists of lowercase English letters only.
