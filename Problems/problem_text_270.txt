1344. Angle Between Hands of a ClockMedium934197Add to ListShareGiven two numbers, hour and minutes, return the smaller angle (in degrees) formed between the hour and the minute hand.
Answers within 10-5 of the actual value will be accepted as correct.

Example 1:

Input: hour = 12, minutes = 30
Output: 165

Example 2:

Input: hour = 3, minutes = 30
Output: 75

Example 3:

Input: hour = 3, minutes = 15
Output: 7.5


Constraints:

1 <= hour <= 12
0 <= minutes <= 59
